Risk, compliance & audit readiness

We help you understand your current security posture, align with international and local regulations, and build an action plan that regulators and stakeholders can trust.

  • Security risk assessments for airports, airlines and cargo facilities.
  • Gap analysis against ICAO Annex 17, national programs and operator SOPs.
  • Corrective action plans, documentation and internal audit preparation.

On-the-ground operations & team training

From access control and screening to insider threat and emergency procedures, we design and train operational teams to respond consistently and effectively.

  • SOP design for checkpoints, landside/airside access and baggage security.
  • Scenario-based training and recurrent certification for AvSec staff.
  • Tabletop exercises and live drills with multi-agency coordination.
  • Performance monitoring and mystery passenger programs.

Digital & cybersecurity for aviation

  • Protection of airport and airline critical IT/OT systems.
  • Identity, access and data protection for operations and passengers.
  • Continuous monitoring and incident response playbooks.

Frequently asked questions

If you work in aviation security or operations in Central America, these are some of the questions we hear most often.

Are you focused only on Central America?

Yes. Our primary focus is Central America and nearby regional routes. This allows us to deeply understand local threats, regulations, infrastructure and culture, while aligning with global standards.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We work with international and domestic airports, passenger and cargo airlines, ground handlers, cargo terminals, maintenance facilities and government entities involved in aviation security.

Can you help us prepare for an upcoming audit or inspection?

Absolutely. We support operators in reviewing documentation, training teams, performing mock inspections and closing gaps before official visits from authorities or third-party auditors.

Do you provide training in Spanish and English?

Yes. Our consultants deliver training, exercises and documentation in Spanish and English, adapting content to the operational reality of each site and team.

How do you integrate physical and cyber security?

We evaluate both physical and digital assets, identify shared risks (such as access control, identity and information flows) and design unified procedures and responses across departments.

How do we start working with AviaSec?

Start with an introductory call to discuss your current situation and objectives. From there, we propose an initial assessment or quick-win project tailored to your risk profile and deadlines.
